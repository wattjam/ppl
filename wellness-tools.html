Help with Everyday Life

Available to all employees, meQuilibrium is an online, confidential, personalized stress management program that can help you:

  • Face daily pressures with calm and confidence
  • Connect better with friends, family, and coworkers
  • Change thinking patterns that leave you frustrated, anxious, pessimistic or full of shame, panic, and guilt

Here’s how it works:

  1. Register at mymeQ.com/paypal and take your free stress assessment.
  2. Get your meQ profile and score. Knowing your stress personality and its strengths and weaknesses can help you better respond when things go south.
  3. Follow your road map. Use your personalized action plan to start better managing your stress.

Virtual Weight-Loss Management

We all want to live a healthy life. Though each of us may have different goals for our health, taking simple steps in the right direction leads to positive life changes and helps us to achieve these goals. With Real Appeal, you get the support you need to improve your health. Real Appeal is a free* and convenient 52-week online weight-loss program that puts interactive videos, live online group discussions, and personalized coaching at your fingertips.

*Real Appeal is available at no additional cost to all eligible employees, eligible spouses and adult dependents over the age of 18 with a BMI of 23 or greater, regardless if you are enrolled in a medical plan.
