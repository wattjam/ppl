How to Enroll

Annual Enrollment for 2017 begins November 7 and ends November 18, 2016. Take a few moments to look over the information on this page, so you’ll be ready.

Prepare

Before November 7, 2016

  • Learn about your 2017 benefits and think about your needs for next year.
  • Visit http://welcometouhc.com/paypal to learn about our new medical insurance carrier.
  • Go to Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) (@Work | Log In ) to watch a short video about the 2017 changes to your benefits.
  • Compare your 2017 medical plan options.
  • View the Benefits Annual Enrollment Information Event schedule.

Enroll online with Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR)

During Annual Enrollment (November 7–18, 2016)

Take action between November 7 and November 18 to be sure you’ll have the coverage that’s the best fit for you and your family in 2017.

  • Attend a Benefits Annual Enrollment Information Event.
  • Visit Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) — PayPal’s personalized benefits portal and online enrollment system that makes enrolling fast and easy. The site is intuitive and simple to use. It has personalized information targeted to you, and tools to help you compare and choose your benefits.

    You can log on to YBR directly from work or home.

    • On the PayPal network?  Go directly to YBR (@Work ).
    • Off the PayPal network?  Visit www.ybr.com/benefits/paypal (Log In ).

  • New to the YBR site? The first time you log on, you’ll need to follow the prompts to create a user ID and password. Click "Are you a new user?" and follow the prompts to create a user ID and password.
  • Did you forget your YBR password? If you’ve forgotten your user ID or password, click "Forgot User ID or Password?" to retrieve your user ID and reset your password.

When you enroll on YBR, be sure to:

  • Use the online tools on YBR to help you choose your benefits.
  • Make your benefit elections for 2017.
  • Enroll your eligible dependents.
  • Designate your life and accident insurance beneficiaries.
  • Print a Confirmation of Coverage.

Phone: You may also enroll over the phone by calling YBR Customer Service at 844-474-6641.
Customer Care Specialists are available Monday through Friday between 5:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m., Pacific time. You’ll need your password to enroll over the phone.

If you don’t enroll

If you don’t take action during the Annual Enrollment period, you’ll miss your opportunity to change your current benefits or add dependents for 2017. Your current coverage—for the same dependents you’re covering now—will continue for 2017, except for the following:

If you’re currently enrolled or participating in: Here’s what will happen if you don’t take action during Annual Enrollment:
$300 Deductible (PPO 300) or CDHP with HSA You’ll remain enrolled in the option you have now, but the plan will be administered by UnitedHealthcare.
PPO 750 This plan will not be available for 2017. If you’re currently enrolled in the PPO 750 and you don’t select a new plan during Annual Enrollment, you’ll automatically be moved into the UnitedHealthcare $300 Deductible plan with the same coverage category you have now (for example, "Employee Only" or "Employee + Family").
Dental plan You’ll automatically be moved into Option 2, with the same coverage category you have now.
Vision plan You’ll automatically be moved into Option 2, with the same coverage category you have now.
Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs) You must enroll if you want to participate in 2017, even if you’re participating in an FSA today.
Health Savings Account (HSA) If you plan to keep the CDHP with HSA medical plan option for 2017, you will need to activate your new HSA with Optum Bank. In mid-December, you will receive information on how to do that and how to transfer your account balance. If you choose to keep your HSA open at Health Equity, you’ll be required to begin paying the monthly administrative fee beginning in January 2017.

After you enroll

You’ll receive a confirmation statement in December that outlines your benefit choices for 2017. Be sure to review it and contact YBR if anything is incorrect.

If you currently participate in a PayPal Blue Shield plan, you will receive a new UnitedHealthcare plan ID card with a new ID and a new group number (Group # 909006) in mid-December. Visit http://welcometouhc.com/paypal for more information.

ID cards (including dental plan ID cards, if you enroll in a new plan) will arrive at your home address in mid-December. Please use your new ID card beginning January 1, 2017.

Payroll deductions for your share of the cost of your benefits will begin with the first paycheck in January 2017.

Changing your elections

Elections you make during Annual Enrollment will take effect January 1, 2017. You can’t change them during the year unless you experience a life or employment change that qualifies under IRS rules.

Refer to Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) (@Work | Log In ) to see if an event allows you to make a change. If at any time you experience a life status change and you need to update your benefits, you must notify YBR within 31 days of the event.
