2016 Time Off Benefits

PayPal offers a variety of time off benefits to help you enjoy some special moments in your life or just to recharge when you need it.

Leaves of absence

PayPal provides the following paid leave programs:

  • Maternity leave (for birth mothers)
  • Bonding leave (mothers, fathers, same-sex spouses, and domestic partners)
  • Family care leave
  • Sick leave

Go to Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) (@Work | Log In ) to learn more.

Time off programs

PayPal provides tracking-free vacation for exempt employees and paid time off for non-exempt employees. Go to Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) (@Work | Log In ) to see your specific time off program benefits.

Sabbatical

Full- and part-time employees are eligible to request a four-week paid sabbatical after completing five years of continuous service. Generally, the calculation is made based on your hire date with PayPal. The sabbatical must be taken on a continuous basis (all four weeks at once).
