Progyny Fertility Benefits

Pursuing fertility treatment can be complicated, emotionally draining, and expensive. If you are enrolled in a UnitedHealthcare medical plan, Progyny can help you and your family during this very personal journey through services such as egg freezing, IVF, and pre-implantation genetic screening. All employees can receive surrogacy benefits and adoption support, regardless of medical plan coverage.

Who Is Progyny?

Progyny is a specialty fertility vendor with a preferred network and a track record of delivering positive results while significantly reducing the risk of multiple births.

Smart Cycle Fertility Treatments

If you’re a UnitedHealthcare (UHC) plan member, you have access to the Progyny Fertility Smart Cycle Benefits. A Smart Cycle is a complete set of fertility treatments for in-vitro fertilization (IVF), fertility preservation (egg/sperm freezing), or intrauterine insemination/artificial insemination (IUI). Coverage for each Smart Cycle also includes the consultation, diagnostic testing, monitoring and management of the fertility care, preimplantation genetic screening, intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI), embryo cryopreservation, one year of embryo or semen storage, anesthesiology, personalized member education, and concierge service through from a Progyny Patient Care Advocate (PCA.)

There is a lifetime maximum of two Smart Cycles, but there is no benefit dollar limit. You'll have peace of mind in knowing that your Smart Cycle will be completely covered without having to exhaust insurance coverage mid-treatment. Additionally, each Smart Cycle covers diagnostic tests and technologies that are paid out of pocket normally.

Getting Started

Since Smart Cycle provides comprehensive coverage for all fertility treatments, you do not need precertification. This gives doctors the flexibility to provide the most effective treatment, the first time.

Once you have met your UHC medical plan deductible, authorized fertility services will be covered at 90%, and you will be responsible for 10% of the cost until you reach your out-of-pocket maximum.

To begin treatment, you must first contact Progyny at 833-838-5850.

If You’re Currently Receiving Fertility Treatment

We understand that fertility treatment is a comprehensive and lengthy process.

If you started your treatment prior to January 1, 2018 but will not complete your treatment until after January 1, 2018 transition of coverage may be approved for you. Please contact Progyny at 833-838-5850 to speak to a Patient Care Advocate (PCA) who can assist you.

PCAs are available Monday through Friday, from 6:00 a.m.– 6:00 p.m. PT. Your PCA can also help you locate in-network providers for fertility care.
