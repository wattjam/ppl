Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

You are automatically enrolled in the Employee Assistance Program (EAP) through the Optum EAP at no cost to you. The EAP offers professional and confidential counseling services that include referrals for the care of a child or elderly family member, help in managing financial problems, and much more.

You and your family members are eligible for six confidential counseling sessions (by phone or in person) per issue per year at no cost to you. Once you complete your six free sessions, additional sessions may be covered under your medical plan at the specialist rate, and are provided in addition to any services you receive through your medical plan.

Through the EAP, you and your family members can talk to an expert who can help you:

  • Manage stress, anxiety, depression, and addiction
  • Handle relationship issues and divorce
  • Develop your resilience
  • Cope with illness and death
  • Manage your finances and create a financial plan
  • Access legal advice from a licensed attorney

Virtual Mental Health Visits

All employees* have convenient access to therapists through online video conferencing. You can speak with a clinician who can evaluate and treat conditions such as depression and anxiety. They can also provide therapy, and when appropriate, prescribe medications. Click here to learn more about Optum Telemental Health Visits.

*Employees located in Nebraska have access to virtual mental health visits through the Nebraska EAP provider, Arbor.

Get More Help with Everyday Life

Available to all employees, meQuilibrium is an online, confidential, personalized stress management program that can help you find more balance in your life.
