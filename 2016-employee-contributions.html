*Imputed income

The IRS states that the fair market value for domestic partner coverage is taxable to the employee. This means the full cost of individual coverage (for your domestic partner) would be added to your taxable income (imputed income). You will see domestic partner imputed income added to your earnings (to incur the tax liability) and then deducted from your gross pay.
