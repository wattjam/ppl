How to Enroll

Annual Enrollment for 2017 begins on November 7, 2016. Take a few moments to look over the information on this page, so you’ll be ready.

Before November 7, 2016

  • Learn about your 2017 benefits and think about your needs for next year.
  • Visit http://welcometouhc.com/paypal to see if your current doctors are part of the UnitedHealthcare network.

During Annual Enrollment (November 7–18, 2016)

Take action between November 7 and November 18 to be sure you’ll have the coverage that’s the best fit for you and your family in 2017.

  • Visit Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) — PayPal’s personalized benefits portal and online enrollment system that makes enrolling fast and easy. The site is intuitive and simple to use. It has personalized information targeted to you, and tools to help you compare and choose your benefits.
    • New to the YBR site? The first time you log on, you’ll need to follow the prompts to create a user ID and password. Click "Are you a new user?" and follow the prompts to create a user ID and password.
    • Did you forget your YBR password? If you’ve forgotten your user ID or password, click "Forgot User ID or Password?" to retrieve your user ID and reset your password.
  • When you enroll on YBR, be sure to:
    • Make your benefit elections for 2017.
    • Enroll your eligible dependents.
    • Print a Confirmation of Coverage.

Phone : You may also enroll over the phone by calling YBR Customer Service at 844-474-6641.
Customer Care Specialists are available Monday through Friday between 5:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m., Pacific time. You’ll need your password to enroll over the phone.

If you don’t enroll

If you don’t take action during the Annual Enrollment period, here’s what will happen:

  • Your current coverage, for the same dependents you’re covering now, will continue for 2017,* except for:
    • Coverage in the $300 Deductible or CDHP with HSA. You will remain enrolled in the option you have now, but the plan will be administered by UnitedHealthcare.
    • Coverage in the PPO 750. This plan will not be available for 2017. If you’re currently enrolled in the PPO 750 and you don’t select a new plan during Annual Enrollment, you’ll automatically be moved into the UnitedHealthcare $300 Deductible plan with the same coverage category you have now (for example, "Individual Only" or "Individual + Family").
    • Coverage in the dental plan. If you’re currently enrolled in dental coverage and you don’t choose a new coverage option during Annual Enrollment, you’ll automatically be moved into Option 2, with the same coverage category you have now.
    • Coverage in the vision plan. If you’re currently enrolled in vision coverage and you don’t choose a new coverage option during Annual Enrollment, you’ll automatically be moved into Option 2, with the same coverage category you have now.
    • Participation in a Health Savings Account (HSA). If you’re currently enrolled in the CDHP with HSA and you plan to keep that plan for 2017, you will need to activate your new HSA with Optum Bank. In December, you will receive information on how to do that and how to transfer your account balance. If you choose to keep your HSA open at Health Equity, you’ll be required to begin paying the monthly administrative fee beginning in January 2017.

    *Assumes COBRA eligibility continues in January 2017.

After you enroll

You’ll receive a confirmation statement that lists your benefit choices for 2017. Be sure to review it and contact YBR Customer Service if anything is incorrect.

If you currently participate in a PayPal Blue Shield plan, you will receive a new UnitedHealthcare plan ID card with a new ID and a new group number (Group # 909006). For website access, you will need to register on the UnitedHealthcare site, http://welcometouhc.com/paypal. ID cards will arrive at your home address in mid-December. Please use your new ID beginning January 1, 2017.

Changing your elections

Elections you make during Annual Enrollment will take effect January 1, 2017. You can’t change them during the year unless you experience a life or employment change that qualifies under IRS rules.

Refer to Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) to see if an event allows you to make a change. If at any time you experience a life status change and you need to update your benefits, you must notify YBR within 31 days of the event.
