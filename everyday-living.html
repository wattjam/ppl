Virtual Weight-loss Management

We all want to live a healthy life. Though each of us may have different goals for our health, taking simple steps in the right direction leads to positive life changes and helps us to achieve these goals. Real Appeal is a free* and convenient 52-week online weight-loss program that puts interactive videos, live online group discussions, and personalized coaching at your fingertips.

*Real Appeal will be available at no additional cost to all eligible employees, eligible spouses and adult dependents over the age of 18 with a BMI of 23 or greater, regardless if you are enrolled in a medical plan.
