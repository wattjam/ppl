Educational Assistance Program

The Educational Assistance Program supports continuing education and developmental programs that can be applied to your current role or reasonably likely future role with the company, including tuition, books and lab expenses for courses pre-approved by your manager. You must receive a passing grade of C or better for undergraduate courses, or a B or better for graduate courses. Expenses of up to $5,250 per calendar year are covered. Contact MyHR for additional information on the PayPal Educational Assistance Program.
