Life, Accident and Disability Insurance

These benefits provide you and your family with a measure of financial protection if you become disabled and can't work, or in the case of severe accidental injury or death.

Basic life and accidental death and dismemberment (AD&D) insurance, business travel accident insurance and disability insurance are provided by the company at no cost to you. You can purchase optional life and AD&D insurance for yourself and your eligible dependents—you can find the premiums for these on Your Benefits ResourcesTM (YBR) (@Work | Log In ).

Life Insurance (Provided by Prudential)
Plan Coverage description Cost
Basic Life Insurance Twice your annual earnings (including commissions) to a maximum of $2 million. Company-provided
Optional Life Insurance One to 6 times base salary, up to a maximum of $2 million. Medical evidence of insurability (EOI) is required for policies equal to 3 times salary or $500,000 (whichever is less). Post-tax deduction—premiums are based on amount selected and tobacco status. Premiums available on YBR.
Spouse Optional Life Insurance Up to the lesser of $250,000 or 50% of employee coverage. Medical evidence of insurability (EOI) is required for policies greater than $75,000. Post-tax deduction—premiums are based on amount selected and tobacco status. Premiums available on YBR.
Child Optional Life Insurance Up to $25,000 per child. Post-tax deduction—premiums are available on YBR.

Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance (Provided by AC Newman)
Plan Coverage description Cost
Basic AD&D Insurance Twice your annual earnings (including commissions) to a maximum of $2 million. Company-provided
Optional employee-only or employee and family AD&D Insurance Up to 6 times annual earnings (including commissions) to a maximum of $2 million. Post-tax deduction—premiums are based on amount selected and tobacco status. Premiums available on YBR.

Disability Insurance (Provided by Prudential)
Plan Coverage description
Short-Term Disability (STD) The company-provided STD program provides salary continuation of up to 80% of your base salary, up to a maximum of $6,500 per week for up to 6 months. There is a 7-day waiting period for benefits.
Long-Term Disability (LTD) The company-provided LTD program provides benefits of 67% of your base salary, up to $25,000 per month.

Business Travel Accident (BTA) Insurance

Business travel accident insurance and emergency travel assistance are available while you're traveling on behalf of the company. The policy provides life and accident insurance of up to five times your salary ($1 million limit), insurance for medical expenses incurred outside your home country and insurance for lost baggage and cash or cash equivalents.
