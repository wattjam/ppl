Giving Programs

We’ve made it even easier to give back by lowering the amount required to receive a matching gift from us. Give as little as $10 to a nonprofit and PayPal will match it, dollar for dollar, up to $2,500 per year. Plus, when you volunteer your time, we’ll give $10 for every hour you donate, up to $500, to the nonprofit of your choice. For instance, if you’ve volunteered with Kiva, you can log your hours and receive funds to donate to Kiva OR any organization you choose.

Give Time Off (GTO)

Through our charitable giving initiative, PayPal GIVES, you have the opportunity to be paid 100% of your base pay for eight hours per year while volunteering at a qualifying charitable organization. GTO is available after your first year of employment.

Visit the PayPal Gives site today to:

  • Request matching gifts
  • Log volunteer hours
  • Search and sign up for volunteer activities
  • Post volunteer and fundraising opportunities so others can join
  • Learn more about other programs, as well as skills-based opportunities offered by the Social Innovation team
