Additional Benefits

PayPal provides a variety of additional benefits to employees.

  • Assistance programs
  • Reimbursement programs

Assistance programs

Care.com: Are you looking for ongoing or occasional child care, someone to care for your pet while you are on vacation or perhaps a tutor for your child? Care.com is a service that helps families find ongoing, occasional and back-up care for the entire family by providing a network of caregivers and access to references, reviews and background checks. Find out how Care.com can help you find a caregiver. To activate your complimentary membership, access Care.com at paypal.care.com. Simply click on the orange "Join now" button. Initially, you'll need to activate your account using your work email address. Your Care.com profile can be changed later to your preferred email address.

Pet Insurance is available to you on a voluntary basis to provide coverage for veterinary care for your household pets, such as dogs, cats and birds. Employees receive a group discount of 5% under the plan.

Stanford Health Navigator Services is a customized web portal where all employees and their families can access specialist and secondary health resources, services and amenities. From this site, you can also access the world-renowned Stanford Health Library, which provides scientifically based medical information to help you make informed decisions about health care. Stanford Health Navigators are also available by phone to answer any questions and provide additional support and resources at times of important health care needs. Navigators can help with scheduling appointments and coordinating specialist visits at Stanford Hospitals and Clinics. Stanford Health Navigator Services are intended to complement the role of your current health care provider and primary care physician. You can reach Stanford Health Navigator Services online at https://stanfordhealthcare.org/partners/paypal.html, by email at paypalnavigator@stanfordhealthcare.org, or by telephone at 844-463-7366844-463-7366 (outside the U.S.: +1-650-736-2741+1-650-736-2741).

Reimbursement programs

Adoption Assistance Plan provides up to $10,000 per adoption to help pay for qualified expenses related to the adoption of a child, including attorney fees, court costs and adoption fees.

Educational Assistance Program supports continuing education and developmental programs that can be applied to your current role or reasonably likely future role with the company, including tuition, books and lab expenses for courses pre-approved by your manager. You must receive a passing grade of C or better for undergraduate courses, or a B or better for graduate courses. These expenses are covered up to a maximum of $5,250 per calendar year.
