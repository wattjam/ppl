Care.com

Are you looking for ongoing or occasional child care, someone to care for your pet while you are on vacation, or perhaps a tutor for your child? Care.com is a service that helps families find ongoing, occasional and back-up care for the entire family by providing a network of caregivers and access to references, reviews and background checks. Find out how Care.com can help you find a caregiver. To activate your complimentary membership, access Care.com at www.paypal.care.com. Simply click on the orange "Join now" button. Initially, you'll need to activate your account using your work email address. Your Care.com profile can be changed later to your preferred email address.
