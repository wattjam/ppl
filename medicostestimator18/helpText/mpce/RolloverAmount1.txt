If you participated in the Consumer Directed Health Plan (CDHP) with Health Savings Account (HSA) option in 2017, please select your estimated HSA carryover for 2018, if any. Keep in mind, if you don't use all of your current year's HSA funds, what remains will roll over to your HSA for the following plan year.
