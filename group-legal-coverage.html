Group Legal Coverage

Hyatt Legal Plans (a MetLife® company) offers you and your family value, convenience, and the comfort of knowing you can access legal services for almost all personal legal matters. It gives you easy and low-cost access to a wide variety of personal legal services.

You can purchase group legal coverage for just under $8 per pay period. Employees may enroll in this plan during the initial new hire enrollment period or during the planâ€™s Annual Enrollment, usually held in the fall each year.

Group Legal Coverage Benefits
  • Access to more than 11,000 network attorneys. All plan attorneys have met stringent selection criteria and average 25 years or more of legal experience.
  • No additional attorney fees for covered legal services from plan attorneys. Or, you may be reimbursed for covered services from non-plan attorneys according to a set fee schedule.
  • No deductibles, copays or claim forms.

Covered Services

A partial list of covered services includes:

  • Consumer protection
  • Debt and financial matters
  • Defense of civil lawsuits
  • Document preparation and review
  • Elder law matters
  • Traffic offenses (excludes DUI)
  • Wills and estate planning
  • Family law
  • Immigration assistance
  • Juvenile court defense
  • Real estate matters
  • Trusts
