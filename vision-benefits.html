Vision Benefits

In addition to protecting your eyesight, regular eye exams can also lead to early detection of other diseases, like diabetes and high blood pressure. PayPal provides a choice of vision benefits through VSP:

  • The Core option covers routine eye exams and provides a standard frame and lens benefit. The Core option does not cover LASIK surgery.
  • The Enhanced option offers two benefits for frames and lenses, and contact lenses, and includes a $1,000 allowance toward LASIK surgery.

Please note: For the Enhanced option, there is a $150 allowance for glasses or contacts. You have $150 to spend on contacts or glasses using the first and second pair benefit. You can received two pairs of glasses, glasses and contacts, or contacts totaling $300 (using $150 under the 1st & 2nd pair).
